Allow me to share a bit about my background:

After graduating with my Bachelor’s in Health Services Management in 2018, I continued to pursue my education by receiving my Master’s in Health Informatics (Data Analytics concentration) from UAB in 2020. At UAB, I graduated first in my class with a 4.0 GPA and was recognized as “a student who shows promise in the health informatics field” with the faculty-selected William W. At RISC, in a highly independent and remote work setting, I analyzed a research database and the department’s workflow to create a Data Management Plan (see Project #1).

I’ve spent the last few years intensely focused on my education, and am now prepared to channel that same energy into my career.
